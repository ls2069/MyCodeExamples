What are Discontinued LEGO® sets?

To put it simply, Discontinued LEGO® sets are designs, pieces, and entire sets that were retired for any number of reasons!

At Discontinued LEGO®, we strive to display classic sets that still hold sentimental value to all who may have previously owned, seen, or played with the retired sets at any stage in their life

To learn more about our collection, click the button below to explore the classical pieces and dive right into a world of nostalgic entertainment!


Our Collection

What sets do we have?

Although LEGO® has been producing plastic toys since 1963, our collection includes sets spanning from the early 2000s to the late 2010s

We only have these in our collection as sets prior to the early 2000s tend to be in poor condition, meaning several parts are missing and the nostalgic value is lost!
